description: "Additive electronics manufacturing — 3D printed PCBs, embedded passive components, conformal circuits, and custom printed sensors."

process:
  - step: "01"
    title: "Design Review"
    text: "We review your schematic or layout file and assess printability — trace width, via requirements, component integration, and substrate selection. Design-for-print recommendations before production."
  - step: "02"
    title: "Ink & Substrate Selection"
    text: "Silver, copper, or carbon ink selected for your electrical specification. Substrate chosen for the application — FR4, flexible PET, textile, or 3D-formed polymer."
  - step: "03"
    title: "Print & Sinter"
    text: "Aerosol jet or inkjet deposition of conductive traces. Dielectric layers between conductive layers for multi-layer boards. Photonic or thermal sintering to target resistivity."
  - step: "04"
    title: "Component Integration"
    text: "Passive components embedded during print or placed and soldered post-print. Active components placed via pick-and-place or manual mounting."
  - step: "05"
    title: "Test & Deliver"
    text: "Continuity testing, resistance measurement, and functional verification. Delivered with process documentation and material certificates."
