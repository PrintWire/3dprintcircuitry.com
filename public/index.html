Applications

Electronics Printing Use Cases

IoT & Sensor Devices

Custom sensor integration with antennas, power traces, and digital logic on a single printed substrate. No standard PCB layout constraints.

Wearables & Body Electronics

Flexible and stretchable circuits conforming to body geometry. Biosensing electrodes, ECG patches, and motion sensors printed on wearable substrates.

Aerospace & Defence

Conformal antennas on aerodynamic surfaces. Weight-optimised circuits with embedded passives eliminating discrete component mass.

Medical Devices

Biocompatible printed circuits for implantable and disposable diagnostic devices. Single-use biosensor arrays and lab-on-chip substrates.

What We Offer

Electronics Printing Services

From a single prototype PCB to conformal antennas on production housings — additive electronics at every scale.

🖨️

3D Printed PCBs

Aerosol jet and inkjet-printed conductive traces. Multi-layer boards with blind and buried vias — no drilling, no etching.

⚙️

Embedded Components

Resistors, capacitors, and inductors printed directly into the substrate. Component values tunable post-print via laser trimming.

🌀

Conformal Electronics

Circuits on curved, flexible, and 3D-formed substrates. Antennas on product surfaces, strain sensors on structural geometry.

🔬

Printed Sensors

Custom piezoresistive, capacitive, and electrochemical sensors printed to specification. Rapid iteration from schematic to functional device.
