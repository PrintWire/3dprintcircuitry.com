What We Cover

🖨️

3D Printed PCBs

Aerosol jet and inkjet-printed conductive traces on flat and 3D substrates. Silver nanoparticle ink at 4–6 µΩ·cm. Multi-layer boards with blind/buried vias — no drilling, no etching.

⚙️

Embedded Component Printing

Resistors, capacitors, and inductors printed directly into the circuit substrate. Eliminates discrete component placement for space-constrained IoT and wearable designs.

🌀

Conformal & 3D Electronics

Circuits on curved, flexible, and 3D-formed substrates. Antennas on product housing, strain sensors on structural geometry, biosensing electrodes on wearable forms.

🔬

Printed Sensors

Custom sensor arrays from piezoresistive carbon ink, PEDOT:PSS electrodes, and stretchable conductors. Functional from schematic to tested device in 24 hours.
